## Rating Guide

- S: Cleared with 3+ turns remaining, stable route, low damage.
- A: Cleared with 1-2 turns remaining, route is understandable.
- B: Cleared on the final allowed turn or with heavy damage.
- C: Cleared, but route feels brittle, unclear, or overly dependent on enemy movement.
- Failed: Could not clear within reasonable attempts.
